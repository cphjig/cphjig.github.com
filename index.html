The Idea

CPH-JIG is a community where we do regular meet-ups in the Copenhagen area, for people with an interest in JVM related stuff. Our interest are both "full stack" – meaning everything from type theory to mechanical sympathy and JVM internals – and polyglot in the sense of any programming language that runs on or is somehow involved with the JVM. We welcome a broad range of topics, and speakers volunteer to talk about cool experiments they've done, open source projects they've worked on or anything else that is interesting and JVM related, that they would like to share.

  • The meet-up starts at 17:00
  • Presentations can be anywhere from 15 minutes to an hour long
